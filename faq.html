FAQs




What is Innovation Geeks?


Innovation Geeks is website which helps to the people who works in cottage industry by selling their products without middle man



What is the motivation behind this idea?


The motivation behind this is normally the people who works in cottage industry don't get profit through selling their products due to middle man.This website helps to sell their products directly without middle man.



Can I now place orders using Pay on Delivery


We are now accepting Cash/Pay on Delivery in most locations where we are delivering all products. If your location is eligible for Pay on Delivery, you will see the option on the payment page while placing your order. In locations where we are delivering only essential products, we will continue to accept pre-paid orders only.



I have already placed an order, but it is showing a long delivery date?


We are gearing up our operations to start delivering all products across the country in all zones following the Central and respective State Government guidelines. Hence, deliveries may take longer than usual. We appreciate your patience in this regard.
